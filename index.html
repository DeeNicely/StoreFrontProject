It's approaching thunderstorm weather season, which can mean high anxiety for Fido. A few tips to ease your dogs stress include having a safe zone where he/she feels comfortable, a Thunderjacket or weighted blanket often times helps, play soothing music or have a TV on for background noise, and if nothing else works, there are nutritional and herbal supplements that can help.

Services Offered

Boarding

We offer a variety of boarding options from overnight visits to VIP packages. You may also add daycare or play packages to enhance the experience. Your beloved pup will get lots of love and attention!

Grooming Services

We have a staff of experienced groomers at the ready for whatever your dog may need from nail trims to full spa day treatments.

Training

We offer plenty training choices to fit your needs. We have group training, private lessons, or multi-day overnight bootcamps. All of our trainers are Good Citizen certified and trained in animal CPR.

Organic Treats

We carry homemade organic treats made by local dog bakery Pupcakes. Special order daily treats, special occasion cookies, or cupcakes.

Daycare

Give your dog some fun time and excercise while your busy. We offer group play according to size and temperment. Private play is also an option for senior dogs, dogs with anxiety or disabilities.

Homemade Meals

Do you have a picky eater, a dog with food allergies, or just looking to optimize your pups overall health? We have partnered with local Three Dog Kitchen to provide wholesome, tasty, nutritious meals that are available by special order. Meals are available to our guests that are boarding with us.
